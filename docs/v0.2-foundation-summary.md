# v0.2 Foundation Release Summary

This release establishes the historical and political baseline for the postwar AI RPG.

## Included

- World history foundation
- Expanded canon and postwar timeline
- Detailed campaign-start world state
- Nation and faction political framework
- Foundation retrieval index
- Version and changelog files

## Campaign Premise

The game begins six years after Ozai's defeat. The youngest central characters are eighteen, all romanceable characters must be adults, and the future after the animated finale is unwritten.

## Next Foundation Iterations

Future v0.2.x packages should add culture, economy, relationships, families, corruption, reputation, travel, combat, and bending-system foundations before the full character encyclopedia is expanded.
