# v0.2.1 — Factions Expansion

This milestone establishes the primary faction model used by the postwar simulation.

## Added or expanded

- faction-wide membership and reputation rules;
- Team Avatar Network;
- Order of the White Lotus;
- Kyoshi Warriors;
- Dai Li;
- Fire Nation Royal Government;
- Fire Nation Military;
- Southern Water Tribe Council;
- Northern Water Tribe Court;
- Earth Kingdom Royal Administration.

## Required faction data

Each faction defines:

- organizational nature and composition;
- ordinary and crisis activities;
- command structure and internal blocs;
- recruitment and admission requirements;
- ranks or functional roles;
- leaving, suspension, discipline, and expulsion;
- named character affiliations;
- player access and advancement;
- AI simulation constraints.

## Runtime rule

Load the current location's relevant faction file, any faction directly involved in the active quest, and the profiles of named characters making decisions. Faction reputation does not replace individual relationships, legal status, rank, or local jurisdiction.

## Continuity rule

Animated-series affiliations remain binding. All postwar reforms, appointments, legal systems, and organizational disputes are game-original defaults and may change through persistent campaign state.
