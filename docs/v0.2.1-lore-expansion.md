# v0.2.1 Lore Expansion

This milestone expands five previously abbreviated lore chapters into simulation-ready reference documents.

## Expanded files

- `lore/canon_boundary.md`
- `lore/cultures.md`
- `lore/economy.md`
- `lore/metaphysics.md`
- `lore/technology.md`

## Scope

The expansion adds:

- a formal hierarchy for canon, inference, repository defaults, and campaign inventions;
- strict exclusion of *The Legend of Korra* and post-series comic continuity;
- regional and class variation within all four national cultures;
- mixed identities, family structures, gender roles, ritual, hospitality, and postwar cultural change;
- regional economies, trade, labor, property, debt, black markets, and player business systems;
- detailed rules for the Avatar, Avatar State, spirits, Spirit World, energybending, celestial effects, death, visions, and spiritual quests;
- technological baselines, transportation, communications, medicine, industry, weapons, metalbending applications, environmental effects, and invention limits.

## Runtime recommendation

Load `lore/canon_boundary.md` in every campaign session.

Load the other files when scenes involve cultural interaction, trade, supernatural events, inventions, industry, travel infrastructure, or regional world simulation.

## Continuity rule

All material remains on the shared branch `agent/main-characters-v0.2.1`. Future milestones should continue to build on this branch so the final export contains the complete knowledge base.
