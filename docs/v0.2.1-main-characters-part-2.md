# v0.2.1 — Main Characters Part 2

This milestone expands the remaining central character profiles to the same simulation standard as Part 1.

## Expanded profiles

- `characters/main/Iroh.md`
- `characters/main/Azula.md`
- `characters/main/Mai.md`
- `characters/main/Ty_Lee.md`
- `characters/main/Suki.md`
- `characters/main/Ozai.md`

## Coverage

Each profile now includes:

- detailed appearance, clothing, voice, posture and body language;
- animated-series biography and postwar situation;
- personality, values, wounds, goals and speech patterns;
- combat, bending or nonbending capabilities and limitations;
- relationship-specific behavior and knowledge boundaries;
- adult romance, marriage and family considerations where appropriate;
- daily rhythm and independent obligations;
- corruption level, vulnerabilities, warning signs, dark trajectory and redemption path;
- forbidden characterization jumps;
- AI portrayal rules and quest hooks.

## Important safeguards

- Azula cannot be instantly redeemed, cured through romance or reduced to a caricature.
- Ozai remains politically dangerous without firebending and is not a normal romance candidate.
- Iroh is wise but neither omniscient nor free from accountability for his military past.
- Mai's silence is not automatic consent, and she remains independent of Zuko.
- Ty Lee's social intuition is not supernatural mind reading.
- Suki remains an independent commander rather than only Sokka's romantic partner.

## Runtime guidance

Load these profiles together when relationships overlap. Fire Nation royal-family scenes should normally load Zuko, Azula, Ozai and Iroh, plus Mai where relevant. Kyoshi Warrior scenes should load Suki and Ty Lee together with the faction and location files.
