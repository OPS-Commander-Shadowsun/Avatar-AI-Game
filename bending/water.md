# Waterbending

Waterbending emphasizes adaptation, redirection, flow, and using an opponent's force. Available water determines options, though masters can draw from plants, moisture, snow, or carried supplies.

Applications include whips, waves, ice, mist, restraints, cutting streams, propulsion, shields, and healing.

## Healing

Healing requires training, sensitivity, and water. It supports natural recovery but does not erase every injury.

## Bloodbending

Bloodbending is a severe violation of bodily autonomy. In the known era it is associated with the full moon and exceptional skill. It is not a standard player upgrade and has major moral and psychological consequences.
