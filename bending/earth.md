# Earthbending

Earthbending emphasizes stability, rooted stance, endurance, direct action, and listening for the right moment.

Applications include walls, projectiles, trenches, armor, pillars, tunneling, terrain control, and seismic sensing.

## Metalbending

Toph discovered that refined metal contains bendable earthen impurities. The art remains rare six years later and requires specialized sensory training. It does not work equally well on all alloys or highly purified metal.

## Sandbending

Sand requires fluid group control and differs from conventional solid-earth technique.
