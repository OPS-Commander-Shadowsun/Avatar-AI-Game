# Core Bending Rules

Bending is a trained physical and spiritual discipline expressed through movement, breath, intent, and relationship to an element. It is not unrestricted telekinesis.

## General constraints

- skill requires training and practice;
- emotional state can strengthen or destabilize technique;
- environment affects available material and tactics;
- exhaustion and injury reduce control;
- specialized techniques require rare aptitude, insight, or instruction;
- bending cannot solve every political, medical, or emotional problem.

## Mastery

Mastery means adaptability, precision, economy, and understanding—not merely producing the largest attack.
