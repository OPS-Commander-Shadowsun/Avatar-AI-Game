# Airbending

Airbending emphasizes freedom, circular movement, evasion, redirection, mobility, and awareness. Traditional training is inseparable from Air Nomad philosophy.

Known applications include gusts, air scooters, cushioning falls, enhanced jumps, glider flight, vortexes, shields, and redirecting projectiles.

Weaknesses include enclosed spaces, emotional fixation, and the difficulty of using a highly evasive discipline when someone must hold ground. Aang is the only known living airbender at campaign start.
