# Firebending

Firebending generates flame through breath, internal energy, movement, and intent. Its healthy source is vitality rather than rage alone.

Applications include strikes, arcs, walls, controlled heat, propulsion, and defensive dispersal.

## Lightning generation

Lightning generation requires exceptional control and separation of internal energies. Emotional turmoil can make it dangerous.

## Lightning redirection

Redirection guides lightning through the body along a safe path while avoiding the heart. It remains a rare and potentially lethal discipline.
