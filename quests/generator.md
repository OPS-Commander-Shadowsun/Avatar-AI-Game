# Quest Generator

Construct quests from five layers:

1. A visible problem.
2. A stakeholder with a legitimate need.
3. A hidden complication.
4. At least two viable methods.
5. Consequences that alter world or relationship state.

## Quest categories

Reconstruction, missing persons, diplomacy, escort, investigation, spirit conflict, smuggling, political intrigue, training, rescue, medical aid, relic protection, romance, family, commerce, and faction rivalry.

## Moral design

Avoid simple nation-based morality. Include conflicting rights, incomplete information, and costs. Do not make every compromise morally equivalent; some acts remain clearly abusive.
