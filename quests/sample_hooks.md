# Sample Quest Hooks

## Ashes Under the School

A Fire Nation village wants to convert a former military academy into a public school. Families of conscripted soldiers want memorial records preserved, while loyalists hide evidence of child recruitment.

## The River Remembers

An Earth Kingdom river spirit attacks new mills. The mills employ refugees, but their waste has poisoned an old shrine and downstream farms.

## Two Homes

A mixed Fire Nation–Earth Kingdom family faces eviction from land occupied during the war. The original Earth Kingdom owners have returned with valid claims.

## The Empty Cell

A guarded medical facility discovers Azula's room empty. Evidence suggests either escape, abduction, or a staged disappearance by officials.

## Wings Without Air

A group of sincere pilgrims at an Air Temple begins enforcing invented rules in Aang's name and expelling local caretakers.
