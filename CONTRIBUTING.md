# Contributing to the Avatar AI RPG World Bible

Thank you for improving this unofficial, non-commercial worldbuilding project. Contributions should make campaigns more consistent, playable, and respectful of the source material while preserving an open future after the animated finale.

## Before writing

Read `lore/canon_boundary.md`, `lore/world_state.md`, `docs/CONTENT_GUIDE.md`, and the relevant schema. Search for existing claims before adding a date, relationship, institution, ability, or location. Prefer expanding one authoritative page over duplicating a rule.

## Content categories

- **Animated canon:** directly supported by the original animated series.
- **Campaign default:** an original postwar assumption used when a campaign does not override it.
- **Generated campaign state:** an event true only in a particular playthrough.
- **Rumor or belief:** an in-world claim that may be incomplete or false.

Do not import later comics or *The Legend of Korra* as binding facts. Inspiration must be rewritten for this repository's independent continuity and must not predetermine the future.

## Writing standards

Use clear English, descriptive headings, short paragraphs, and lists for reference data. Summarize rather than reproduce scripts or dialogue. Add playable details: motives, constraints, sensory cues, conflicts, resources, schedules, and consequences. Keep narrator knowledge separate from character knowledge.

## Pull request checklist

- Links and filename casing are correct.
- New entities follow the relevant schema.
- Ages, travel times, relationships, and political claims match related files.
- Romance involves adults, consent, and agency.
- Corruption is gradual and includes resistance and recovery.
- No scripts, transcripts, or long copyrighted quotations are included.
- Original material is identified as a campaign default.
