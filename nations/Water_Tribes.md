# Water Tribes

The Northern and Southern Water Tribes share ancestry and bending traditions but differ in resources, institutions, and recent history.

## Northern Tribe

Large ice city, formal leadership, established masters, strong defenses, and pressure to reform restrictive customs.

## Southern Tribe

Smaller but rebuilding rapidly after raids and war losses. Leadership is more improvisational, and outside assistance may create dependence or cultural tension.

## Quest themes

Reconstruction, fishing rights, missing wartime prisoners, healing missions, family reunification, cultural reform, and polar spirits.
