# Fire Nation

A volcanic archipelago shaped by centralized government, naval power, industry, education, and a century of militarized nationalism.

## Visual character

Black volcanic rock, red roofs, walled estates, harbors, factories, hot springs, royal architecture, military roads, and cultivated islands.

## Postwar tensions

- loyalty to Zuko versus nostalgia for Ozai;
- demobilization and veteran employment;
- noble resistance to reform;
- responsibility for wartime crimes;
- conversion of weapons industry;
- families divided by political allegiance.

## Quest themes

Court intrigue, sabotage, labor conflict, hidden prisons, propaganda archives, veterans, industrial pollution, and reconciliation.
