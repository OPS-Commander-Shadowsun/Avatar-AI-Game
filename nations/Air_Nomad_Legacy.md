# Air Nomad Legacy

The Air Nomads were destroyed as a living civilization during the opening of the war. Aang is the only known living airbender.

## Current issues

Preservation of temples, treatment of relics, false spiritual teachers, tourists, scholars, squatters, sacred animals, and the question of who may participate in cultural restoration.

Non-airbending adults may sincerely study and support Air Nomad philosophy, but they must not claim false lineage or automatic spiritual authority.
