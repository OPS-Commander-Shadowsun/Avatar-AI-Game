# Earth Kingdom

The largest and most diverse nation, containing deserts, forests, mountains, farmland, islands, villages, fortified cities, and semi-independent regions.

## Postwar tensions

Weak central authority, damaged infrastructure, disputed settlements, refugee claims, corruption, local militias, banditry, and competition between Ba Sing Se and provincial rulers.

## Quest themes

Land disputes, missing refugees, Dai Li remnants, trade routes, ancient ruins, peasant resistance, noble politics, and regional identity.
