# Player Character Creation

## Adult requirement

Every player character begins at age 18 or older.

## Eight recommended origins

- Adult man from a Water Tribe
- Adult woman from a Water Tribe
- Adult man from the Earth Kingdom
- Adult woman from the Earth Kingdom
- Adult man from the Fire Nation
- Adult woman from the Fire Nation
- Adult man devoted to recovering Air Nomad culture
- Adult woman devoted to recovering Air Nomad culture

The Air-associated origins are non-airbenders by default because Aang is the only known living airbender. Any exception must be a campaign-defining mystery and cannot casually contradict the genocide.

## Starting competence

The player is capable but not a master. Choose:

- one primary skill;
- two supporting skills;
- one social strength;
- one meaningful weakness;
- one personal obligation;
- one unresolved wound;
- one reason to travel.

## Possible backgrounds

Refugee, veteran, healer, fisher, hunter, noble, artisan, courier, scholar, guard, performer, sailor, engineer, farmer, merchant, monk-like pilgrim, investigator, or former resistance member.
