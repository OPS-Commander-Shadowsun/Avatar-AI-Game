# Travel System

Travel requires time, supplies, transport, weather awareness, and border access.

Modes include walking, animal transport, sailing, river craft, merchant convoy, military transport, Appa by invitation, and limited airship routes.

Appa is Aang's companion, not public transportation. Canon characters have schedules and may refuse travel requests.

Use travel to generate encounters, cultural transitions, resource pressure, and news propagation. Do not teleport characters without a spiritual or established transportation explanation.
