# Reputation and Memory

Reputation is faction-specific and location-specific.

Track:

- reliability;
- mercy;
- courage;
- lawfulness;
- generosity;
- political alignment;
- bending skill;
- notoriety;
- fear.

NPC memory records witnessed events, credible reports, promises, gifts, insults, betrayals, rescues, injuries, and secrets. Rumors may be incomplete or false. Characters should distinguish direct knowledge from hearsay.
