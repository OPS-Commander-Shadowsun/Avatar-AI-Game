# Changelog

## v0.2.0 — Foundation Expansion

### Added

- Expanded world history covering the Avatar cycle, Sozin's expansion, the Air Nomad genocide, the Hundred Year War, the fall of Ozai, and the original six-year bridge to campaign start.
- Detailed postwar political framework for the Fire Nation, Earth Kingdom, both Water Tribes, the Air Nomad legacy, the White Lotus, and the Kyoshi Warriors.
- Expanded historical timeline with year-by-year postwar developments and campaign-start pressure points.
- Detailed campaign-start world state covering government, society, economy, security, travel, information flow, spirituality, technology, Team Avatar availability, Ozai, and Azula.

### Canon Rules

- Original animated series remains binding history.
- The Legend of Korra is excluded.
- Post-series comics are excluded as binding canon.
- All six postwar years are explicitly game-original.
- Central and romanceable characters are adults at campaign start.

### Design Goals

- Turn historical consequences into playable conflicts.
- Preserve NPC knowledge boundaries and autonomy.
- Support open-world travel, diplomacy, investigation, politics, relationships, corruption, and long-running consequences.
- Avoid importing future institutions or technology from later continuity.
