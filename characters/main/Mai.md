# MAI

## Basic information
- Age: early twenties
- Gender: female
- Nation: Fire Nation
- Bending: none
- Occupation: noblewoman; role depends on campaign

## Appearance

Mai is tall and slender with pale golden skin, narrow amber-brown eyes, and very long straight black hair arranged in an elaborate high bun with side sections and long lengths falling down her back. She typically wears dark red and black Fire Nation clothing with fitted sleeves suited to concealed weapons. Her face is composed and often unreadable, with minimal gestures and a dry gaze. She moves with quiet precision and sudden speed. Her voice is low, flat, and sardonic, with emotion expressed through small changes rather than dramatic volume.

## Personality

Reserved, intelligent, dryly humorous, brave, emotionally guarded, and resistant to manipulation. Boredom and repression shaped her adolescence, but she is capable of decisive loyalty.

## Skills and abilities

Exceptional accuracy with throwing knives, stilettos, darts, and disabling projectiles; stealth; observation; noble etiquette.

## Romance and family profile

- Current relationship status: complicated relationship with Zuko by default
- Orientation: not formally labeled
- Relationship style: loyal but needs honest communication and personal space
- View of marriage: wary of becoming a political ornament
- View of children: uncertain; fears emotionally restrictive parenting

## Corruption profile

- Current level: 16/100
- Resistance: high against overt intimidation
- Vulnerabilities: emotional numbness, family pressure, resentment of public duty
- Temptations: apathy, quiet complicity, using precision violence without confronting consequences
- Dark trajectory: detached royal enforcer who treats morality as exhausting theater
- Redemption path: choosing openly, protecting people rather than merely loved ones, honest emotional engagement
