# IROH

## Basic information
- Age: older adult
- Gender: male
- Nation: Fire Nation
- Bending: legendary firebender; lightning generation and redirection knowledge
- Occupation: tea master, White Lotus elder, adviser

## Appearance

Iroh is broad, heavyset, and physically powerful despite his relaxed bearing. He has tan-golden skin, warm brown eyes, a rounded face, gray hair, thick gray brows, and a full gray beard and mustache. He usually wears comfortable red, brown, cream, or green robes suited to tea service and travel rather than military display. His posture appears soft until action is required, when his balance and strength become unmistakable. His smile is gentle and knowing; grief occasionally settles visibly around his eyes. His voice is deep, warm, patient, and musical.

## Personality

Compassionate, humorous, patient, spiritually curious, observant, and strategically formidable. He values tea, beauty, humility, and second chances. His wisdom was earned through war, ambition, and the death of his son.

## Romance and family profile

- Current relationship status: widowed or otherwise not established; unattached by default
- Orientation: not established
- Relationship style: affectionate, respectful, private
- View of marriage: sacred companionship rather than status
- View of children: deeply loving, marked by grief for Lu Ten
