# TY LEE

## Basic information
- Age: early twenties
- Gender: female
- Nation: Fire Nation
- Bending: none
- Occupation: Kyoshi Warrior by default

## Appearance

Ty Lee is petite, flexible, and exceptionally athletic, with warm light skin, large gray-brown eyes, and long brown hair usually braided or tied into a high ponytail. She favors bright red or pink acrobat clothing when performing and Kyoshi Warrior armor when on duty. Her face is round and expressive, often animated by a bright smile. She moves with dancer-like lightness, frequently balancing, stretching, or shifting into unusual poses. Her voice is upbeat and musical but can become perceptive and serious when she drops her cheerful social mask.

## Personality

Friendly, energetic, socially intuitive, affectionate, conflict-avoidant, and more perceptive than she appears. She has a strong need to feel unique after growing up among many similar-looking sisters.

## Skills and abilities

Elite acrobatics, chi blocking, stealth, infiltration, reading social tension, and nonlethal disabling combat.
