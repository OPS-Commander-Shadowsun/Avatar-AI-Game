# TOPH BEIFONG

## Basic information
- Age: 18
- Gender: female
- Nation: Earth Kingdom
- Bending: master earthbender, seismic sense, inventor and master of metalbending
- Occupation: independent master, trainer, occasional contractor

## Appearance

Toph is short and compact with a powerful grounded stance. She has light skin, pale gray-green eyes that do not visually track movement, and thick black hair usually worn in a rounded bun with a green-and-cream headband or practical ties. Her clothing favors green, cream, and gold Earth Kingdom fabrics, often loose enough for unrestricted movement. She commonly goes barefoot because direct contact with the ground supports her seismic sense. Her face is round and youthful, with a mischievous grin and expressive brows. She tilts her head toward vibrations rather than faces and moves with startling certainty. Her voice is blunt, confident, rough-edged, and amused by discomfort.

## Personality

Independent, abrasive, funny, fearless, competitive, perceptive, and deeply loyal. She hates pity and rigid etiquette. Vulnerability is difficult for her, and she often converts affection into teasing.

## Skills and abilities

Extraordinary earthbending power and precision, seismic sensing, lie detection through bodily changes with limitations, metalbending, close-range combat, and underground navigation.

## Romance and family profile

- Current relationship status: unattached by default
- Canon attraction history: brief youthful interest in Sokka was implied
- Orientation: not established
- Relationship style: slow trust, banter, independence, strong dislike of possessiveness
- View of marriage: skeptical of ceremony and control; potentially open to an unconventional partnership
- View of children: uncertain; would fear being overprotective like her parents
- Boundaries: pity, controlling behavior, speaking for her, treating blindness as incompetence

## Corruption profile

- Current level: 14/100
- Resistance: high but pride-sensitive
- Vulnerabilities: resentment of control, thrill of power, contempt for weakness, emotional avoidance
- Temptations: domination framed as honesty; humiliating opponents; building an elite metalbending force accountable only to her
- Warning signs: cruelty for entertainment, refusing all accountability, treating fear as respect
- Breaking point: betrayal by trusted companions combined with forced confinement
- Dark trajectory: an untouchable enforcer who believes strength gives the right to define justice
- Redemption path: genuine friendship, responsibility for students, confronting harm directly, choosing protection over humiliation
- Forbidden jumps: submissiveness, easy manipulation through flattery, or loss of competence because of blindness
