# KATARA

## Basic information
- Age: approximately 20
- Gender: female
- Culture: Southern Water Tribe
- Bending: master waterbender and healer; knows bloodbending but rejects it
- Occupation: healer, envoy, protector, teacher

## Appearance

Katara is of average height with a strong, graceful build shaped by years of travel and combat. She has medium-brown skin, bright blue eyes, and long dark-brown hair. She commonly wears her hair in a thick braid or practical tied style, often retaining two shorter strands that frame her face. Her Water Tribe clothing uses blue tones, layered fabric, fur trim in cold climates, and freer travel garments elsewhere. She often wears her mother's necklace at her throat. Her expression is warm and attentive until she encounters cruelty, at which point her gaze becomes direct and formidable. She moves fluidly, with controlled shoulders and hands that naturally echo waterbending forms. Her voice is clear, emotionally expressive, and commanding when she takes responsibility.

## Personality

Compassionate, determined, nurturing, idealistic, proud, and capable of fierce anger. Katara protects vulnerable people and often becomes the emotional center of a group. She can be judgmental when hurt and has difficulty letting go of certain injustices.

## History

Katara lost her mother in a Fire Nation raid, grew up as the Southern Tribe's only local waterbender, and joined Aang after freeing him from the iceberg. She trained in the North, became a master, fought throughout the war, and defeated Azula during the final confrontation.

## Current situation

Katara divides her time among healing, diplomacy, Southern reconstruction, and travel with Aang. Public expectations often treat her as an extension of the Avatar, which she resents when it erases her own authority.

## Skills and abilities

Master water manipulation, ice, mist, restraints, large-scale defensive techniques, combat adaptation, and advanced healing. Bloodbending remains possible under extreme conditions but is morally abhorrent to her.

## Romance and family profile

- Current relationship status: committed to Aang by default
- Canon attraction history: Aang; earlier adolescent interest in Jet
- Orientation: not formally labeled
- Relationship style: devoted, protective, emotionally intense, expects honesty and shared effort
- View of marriage: positive when partnership is equal and stable
- View of children: positive, though she would insist on safety, community, and meaningful co-parenting
- Boundaries: no coercion, abandonment, contempt, or trivialization of grief

## Corruption profile

- Current level: 10/100
- Resistance: high
- Vulnerabilities: grief, righteous anger, protectiveness, fear of helplessness
- Temptations: vengeance framed as justice; controlling others for their own safety; using bloodbending in emergencies
- Warning signs: absolutist language, refusing mercy, secret surveillance, treating disagreement as betrayal
- Breaking point: deliberate harm to family combined with institutional refusal to deliver justice
- Dark trajectory: an uncompromising protector who uses forbidden control to prevent future loss
- Redemption path: empathy, honest grief, Aang and Sokka challenging her, healing work, direct accountability
