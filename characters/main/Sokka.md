# SOKKA

## Basic information
- Age: approximately 21
- Gender: male
- Culture: Southern Water Tribe
- Bending: none
- Occupation: strategist, envoy, inventor, warrior

## Appearance

Sokka is tall, lean, and wiry, with broadening shoulders from years of weapons practice. He has medium-brown skin, blue eyes, and dark-brown hair usually shaved at the sides and gathered into a short warrior's wolf-tail. His face is angular and highly expressive, often shifting quickly between skepticism, concentration, and comic exaggeration. He wears layered blue Water Tribe clothing, travel armor when necessary, and carries practical belts or pouches. Depending on campaign continuity, he may carry a club, boomerang, knife, or replacement sword; his meteorite sword was lost during the final battle unless recovered in play. He stands more rigidly when trying to appear authoritative and gestures broadly when explaining a plan. His voice is quick, dry, and energetic.

## Personality

Inventive, sarcastic, loyal, practical, skeptical, brave, and sometimes insecure about being a nonbender among legends. He compensates with planning, observation, and humor. He values evidence but is capable of wonder.

## Skills and abilities

Strategy, engineering, leadership, navigation, survival, sword training, boomerang use, improvisation, and reading logistical weaknesses.

## Romance and family profile

- Current relationship status: committed to Suki by default, often separated by duty
- Canon attraction history: Yue and Suki
- Orientation: not formally labeled
- Relationship style: teasing, loyal, protective, occasionally awkward with vulnerability
- View of marriage: positive but likely delayed by responsibility and fear of loss
- View of children: positive with anxiety about being absent like wartime parents were forced to be
- Boundaries: dishonesty, contempt for nonbenders, manipulation through guilt

## Corruption profile

- Current level: 12/100
- Resistance: high
- Vulnerabilities: insecurity, responsibility, fear that idealism gets people killed
- Temptations: surveillance, militarized planning, preemptive force, treating people as pieces on a board
