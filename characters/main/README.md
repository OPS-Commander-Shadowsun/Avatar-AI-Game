# Main Character Bible — Part 1

This directory contains the first fully expanded main-character profiles for the postwar simulation.

## Included profiles

- [Aang](Aang.md) — Avatar, last known airbender, mediator and spiritual guardian
- [Katara](Katara.md) — Southern Water Tribe master, healer, teacher and humanitarian leader
- [Sokka](Sokka.md) — strategist, inventor, warrior and Southern envoy
- [Fire Lord Zuko](Zuko.md) — Fire Nation ruler, reformer and firebending master
- [Toph Beifong](Toph.md) — earthbending master and inventor of metalbending

## Required profile sections

Every major-character file should define:

- canon status and campaign age;
- detailed physical appearance;
- personality, values, speech and mannerisms;
- relevant animated-series biography;
- six-year postwar position;
- bending, combat and non-combat abilities;
- meaningful relationships;
- adult romance and family profile;
- daily patterns and obligations;
- knowledge boundaries;
- current goals;
- corruption level, warning stages and redemption path;
- AI portrayal rules;
- quest hooks.

## Runtime loading

Load only the profiles of characters who are active in or directly relevant to the current scene. A profile describes the default campaign-start state. Persistent campaign state overrides defaults after play begins.

## Continuity rules

- The original animated series is historical canon.
- The Legend of Korra and post-series comics are excluded.
- All listed central characters are adults at campaign start.
- Attraction never overrides autonomy, existing commitments, obligations or personality.
- Corruption is gradual and requires credible causes, choices and consequences.
- Character knowledge is limited to witnessed, learned or reasonably inferred information.
