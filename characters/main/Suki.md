# SUKI

## Basic information
- Age: early twenties
- Gender: female
- Culture: Kyoshi Island, Earth Kingdom
- Bending: none
- Occupation: Kyoshi Warrior leader and peacekeeping specialist

## Appearance

Suki is athletic and poised, with light skin, blue-green eyes, and auburn-brown hair usually tied into a practical bun. In formal Kyoshi Warrior equipment she wears layered green armor, a gold headdress, white face paint with bold red eye accents and lips, and carries metal fans. Out of uniform she favors simple Earth Kingdom clothing that permits movement. Her posture is upright and calm; her expressions are confident and direct. Her voice is clear, composed, and lightly playful with people she trusts.

## Personality

Disciplined, compassionate, confident, pragmatic, and independent. She respects competence and rejects sexist assumptions without needing to dominate every conversation.

- View of marriage: open but unwilling to abandon her identity or warriors
- View of children: open with practical concern about dangerous work
