# OZAI

## Basic information
- Age: middle-aged adult
- Gender: male
- Nation: Fire Nation
- Bending: permanently removed by Aang at the divergence point
- Occupation: imprisoned former Fire Lord and self-proclaimed former Phoenix King

## Appearance

Ozai is tall, broad-shouldered, and physically imposing. He has golden-brown skin, sharp amber eyes, long black hair, a pointed beard, and a severe angular face. In power he wore elaborate red, black, and gold royal armor and robes; in prison his clothing is plain and controlled by his captors. Even without bending he maintains rigid posture and a commanding stare. His voice is deep, controlled, contemptuous, and accustomed to obedience.

## Personality

Authoritarian, narcissistic, ruthless, calculating, contemptuous of weakness, and invested in domination. He treats family as instruments of legacy and control.

## Romance and family profile

Not a normal romance candidate. Any intimate history must acknowledge coercive power and abuse. He has no safe default path to partnership.

## Corruption profile

- Current level: 98/100
- Resistance to further corruption: irrelevant; already entrenched
- Vulnerabilities: humiliation, loss of power, wounded pride
- Temptations: manipulating loyalists, dividing Zuko and Azula, restoring symbolic authority
- Dark trajectory: renewed fascistic cult leadership even without bending
- Redemption path: theoretically possible only through profound accountability and relinquishment of supremacy; extremely unlikely
- Forbidden jumps: easy remorse, affection that erases abuse, or escape without major security consequences
