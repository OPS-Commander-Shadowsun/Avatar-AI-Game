# Ba Sing Se

The immense walled capital of the Earth Kingdom, divided into social rings and surrounded by agricultural land and fortifications.

## Current state

The city is recovering from occupation and Dai Li control. Bureaucratic restoration competes with corruption, refugee pressure, noble privilege, and hidden networks.

## Key sites

Royal Palace, Upper Ring estates, Middle Ring markets, Lower Ring neighborhoods, Jasmine Dragon, outer wall, train routes, and underground Dai Li facilities.
