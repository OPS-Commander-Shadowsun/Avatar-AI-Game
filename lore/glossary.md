# World Bible Glossary

## Canon status

The provenance of a statement: animated canon, campaign default, generated campaign state, or in-world belief.

## Campaign default

Original material that makes a new game playable. It is true at initialization unless the group selects another premise and is not official franchise canon.

## Front

An evolving conflict or opportunity with actors, stakes, a progress clock, visible signs, and consequences.

## Clock

A bounded progress track for a plan, recovery, journey, investigation, or threat. It advances only when time and causality justify it.

## Knowledge boundary

The limit between narrator truth and what an entity has witnessed, learned, or plausibly inferred.

## Corruption

Gradual erosion or distortion of values through pressure and repeated choices, not an automatic alignment switch.

## World turn

A periodic simulation step advancing relevant plans, travel, recovery, trade, rumors, and scheduled events.
