# Spirits, the Avatar, and Metaphysics

## The Avatar

The Avatar is the reincarnating bridge between human civilization and the Spirit World and can learn all four elements. The Avatar State draws on accumulated spiritual power and past experience, but it is dangerous when triggered by trauma or used without control.

## Spirits

Spirits are not a single species or moral category. Some embody places, patterns, memories, natural forces, or ancient obligations. A spirit may be benevolent, hostile, alien, playful, territorial, or transformed by human damage.

## Spirit World

The Spirit World is not governed by ordinary geography. Emotional state, symbolism, sacred dates, and spiritual sensitivity can influence passage and perception. Physical strength alone is often ineffective there.

## Energybending

Energybending is exceptionally rare and dangerous. Aang's use against Ozai does not make it a common learnable skill. It must never become a routine player upgrade.

## Death and reincarnation

Ordinary human death is consequential and generally final. The Avatar reincarnates, but individual personality is not casually resurrected. Ghostlike encounters should be spiritual, ambiguous, or tied to specific metaphysical circumstances.
