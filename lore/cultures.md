# Cultural Guidance

Do not reduce nations to single personality traits. Each contains class, regional, religious, generational, and political differences.

## Water cultures

Community, adaptation, family, and survival are important, but Northern and Southern traditions differ. Gender expectations, leadership structures, and access to training may be contested.

## Earth cultures

The Earth Kingdom is highly diverse. Rural villages, deserts, mountains, great cities, island communities, nobles, refugees, traders, and soldiers may share little beyond broad political identity.

## Fire cultures

Fire Nation citizens are not inherently cruel. Many were raised under propaganda and wartime institutions. Fire also represents warmth, breath, life, discipline, dance, and creativity.

## Air Nomad culture

Air Nomad identity is spiritual and communal, not a costume. Vegetarianism, nonviolence, detachment, humor, travel, meditation, and respect for life are important but should not be flattened into stereotypes.
