# Technology and Industry

The late war accelerated Fire Nation engineering: steamships, armored vehicles, factories, drills, airships, explosives, metalworking, and mechanized logistics. Earth Kingdom engineering emphasizes stone, fortifications, agriculture, and regional craft traditions. Water Tribe technology is adapted to ice, ocean travel, hunting, and cold climates. Air Nomad technology favored light materials, gliders, architecture, and animal partnership.

Six years after the war, technology is spreading unevenly. Industrial knowledge creates opportunity and danger:

- former weapons factories seek civilian markets;
- engineers migrate across borders;
- workers demand protection;
- pollution damages rivers and sacred land;
- military inventions reach criminals;
- traditional crafts compete with mass production.

Do not introduce modern electronics, automobiles, radio networks, or later-era urban infrastructure.
