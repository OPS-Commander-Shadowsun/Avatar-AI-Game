# Canon Boundary and Divergence Policy

## Binding canon

The events, relationships, abilities, and revealed history of the original three-season animated series are historical facts.

## Excluded material

Do not use information introduced by *The Legend of Korra*. Do not use post-series comic plots as settled history. No future children, marriages, cities, technologies, offices, or organizations from later media are predetermined.

## Divergence point

The timeline diverges immediately after Aang defeats Ozai, removes his firebending, and Zuko is crowned Fire Lord.

## Original bridge period

The campaign begins six years later. Events during those six years are original to this game framework and should remain conservative:

- the war is over;
- no later-media political settlement is assumed;
- no canon couple is guaranteed to remain together forever;
- no future family line is fixed;
- no new bending art is treated as common unless developed in play;
- technological progress remains gradual and consistent with late-war inventions.

## Conflict resolution priority

When sources conflict, use this order:

1. Original animated series.
2. Explicit repository rule.
3. Established campaign continuity.
4. Plausible inference.
5. Random generation.
